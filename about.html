About Us

Edu Checked is an educational part of the Startup, Checked It. We impart industrial and skill-based courses to school-going students. We teach you how to learn, think and succeed. We provide career guidance and help you explore your possibilities. We revolutionize the Education policy of the nation by filling the void that exists between curriculum-based and skill-based learning.

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform the education sector in India by making learning fun, and formulating them to explore the possibilities and even the impossibilities of the domain. And at last making education accessible to all irrespective of their background.

Our Mission

  • To launch an educational program that will help the students and faculties gain experience that will make Bihar a visionary state.
  • To ignite the learning curiosity of children.
  • To help the state achieve new heights by educating teachers on the knacks of imparting education
  • To convert our workforce into a well-learned and practically trained workforce
  • To educate our future workforce in soft skills and industry-based skills
  • To encourage innovative and creative young minds and help them reach their full potential
